Welcome at BLACK HOLE

Black hole is about spreading awareness about Mental health and how our environment and social media have a huge impact on it. Please note that we are nonprofessionals.

DIGITALIZATION

It is a fact that social media is affecting our mental health. We see all sides of life from the superstars who seem to only have good days, to the average people which seem to have a lot of personal problems, but what Black hole really wan’na talk about is the side, of the far internet, which romanticizes having trouble with Mental Health.

We get it you want to make relatable stuff and maybe you yourself suffer of, whatever you post, but you as someone on the internet need to understand what your posts can do with a young person.


Sadly that these are not the worst. There are even some about suicide and selfharm.
